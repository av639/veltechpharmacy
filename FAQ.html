General Questions

How do I create an account with VelTech Pharmacy?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right of our website and fill in the required information including your name, email address, and a secure password. Once you verify your email address, you'll have full access to your account where you can manage prescriptions, set up auto-refills, and view your order history.

How do I transfer my prescription to VelTech Pharmacy?

Transferring your prescription is easy. You can either:

  1. Call us with your prescription information and we'll handle the transfer from your current pharmacy
  2. Use our online prescription transfer tool by providing your current pharmacy details and prescription information
  3. Ask your doctor to send new prescriptions directly to us

Most transfers are completed within one business day.

What are your delivery options and timeframes?

We offer several delivery options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Delivery: 3-5 business days (FREE for all prescription medications)
  • Express Delivery: 1-2 business days ($9.99)
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available in select areas for an additional fee ($14.99)

Delivery times begin once your prescription is verified and processed by our pharmacists.

Prescription Questions

How do I submit a new prescription?

There are several ways to submit a new prescription:

  1. Online: Upload a photo of your prescription through your account dashboard
  2. Mobile App: Use our app to scan your prescription
  3. Fax: Have your doctor fax the prescription to (123) 456-7891
  4. Phone: Call us at (123) 456-7890 and we can contact your doctor for you
Can I get a 90-day supply of my medication?

Yes, we offer 90-day supplies for most maintenance medications when approved by your insurance and prescribed by your doctor. Many of our customers find this option more convenient and cost-effective. To switch to a 90-day supply:

  1. Contact your doctor to request a new prescription for a 90-day supply
  2. Check with your insurance provider about coverage for 90-day supplies
  3. Update your prescription in your account or contact our pharmacy team
How do I set up automatic refills?

Setting up automatic refills ensures you never run out of your important medications:

  1. Log in to your VelTech Pharmacy account
  2. Navigate to "My Prescriptions"
  3. Find the medication you want to auto-refill and click "Enable Auto-Refill"
  4. Set your preferred delivery frequency (we'll remind you before each shipment)
  5. Save your preferences

You can pause or cancel auto-refills at any time through your account.

Payment & Insurance

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods for your convenience:

  • All major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • Health Savings Account (HSA) and Flexible Spending Account (FSA) cards
  • VelTech Pharmacy gift cards
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay
  • Insurance copayments (after we bill your insurance)

All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

How do I know if you accept my insurance?

VelTech Pharmacy accepts most major insurance plans. To verify if we accept your insurance:

  1. Log in to your account and add your insurance information - we'll automatically check coverage
  2. Call our customer service team at (123) 456-7890 with your insurance card ready
  3. Check our list of accepted insurance providers on our Insurance Information page

Even if we don't directly bill your insurance, you can submit receipts for reimbursement from your provider.

Do you offer any discount programs?

Yes! We offer several ways to save on your medications:

  • VelTech Saver Program: Discounts on hundreds of generic medications (starting at $4 for 30-day supply)
  • Senior Discount: 10% off for customers 65+
  • Auto-Refill Discount: 5% off when you enroll in automatic refills
  • Referral Program: Earn $10 credit for each friend who fills a prescription with us

Ask our pharmacists about the best savings options for your specific medications.
