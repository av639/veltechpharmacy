Our Story

Founded in 2010, VelTech Pharmacy has grown from a single neighborhood pharmacy to a trusted healthcare provider with multiple locations across the city. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide personalized, compassionate care to our community.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers with our comprehensive pharmaceutical services, health consultations, and wellness products. Our team of licensed pharmacists and healthcare professionals are dedicated to your wellbeing.

At VelTech, we believe in going beyond just dispensing medications. We take the time to understand your health needs and provide tailored solutions to help you achieve your best health.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional pharmaceutical care through personalized service, clinical expertise, and innovative solutions that improve health outcomes for our patients and community.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare partner in our community, recognized for our commitment to patient care, health education, and advancing the practice of pharmacy.

Our Values

Compassion, Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, and Community. These core values guide every decision we make and every interaction we have with our patients.
